Towards Massively Parallel Motion Planning with Inverse Dynamics 02. OCP Solver
Concerning Dynamics
Forward Dynamics
  • Consider joint torques as control variables
  • Calculate joint accelerations from the equations of motion
  • Integrate to obtain joint velocities and positions
  • Requires inversion of the mass matrix
Inverse Dynamics
  • Consider joint accelerations as control variables
  • Treat torque limits as additional constraints
  • Does not require inversion of the mass matrix
  • Special case: Underactuated Systems
