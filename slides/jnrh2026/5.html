Towards Massively Parallel Motion Planning with Inverse Dynamics 01. Motivation

AHMP: Agile Humanoid Motion Planning with Contact Discovery *

Ioannis Tsikelis, Evangelos Tsiatsianas, Chairi Kiourt, Konstantinos Chatzilygeroudis, Enrico Mingo Hoffman

Bi-level optimization for simultaneous contact and motion planning using whole-body dynamics.

  • Optimizes both contacts and joint trajectories.
  • Cross-Entropy Method (CEM) algorithm for contact sequence optimization.
  • Gradient-based trajectory optimization for motion planning.
  • (CPU) Parallelized evaluation of contact candidates.

*Presented at IEEE Humanoids 2025, Seoul, Korea
