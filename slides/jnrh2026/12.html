Towards Massively Parallel Motion Planning with Inverse Dynamics 02. OCP Solver
The Optimal Control Problem
  • System states and controls discretized along a time horizon and become optimization variables in a (non-linear) optimization problem.
  • Each state-action pair (stages) linked to the following one via dynamics integration constraints.
  • Support for additional constraints such as joint and control limits, contacts or environmental collisions.
  • Numerical methods can be employed to solve the resulting problem.
